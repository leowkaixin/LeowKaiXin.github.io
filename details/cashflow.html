The CashFlow Storyboard displays the net amount of cash and cash-equivalents bring transferred into and out of a business. It comes in 3 forms: Operating, Investing and Financing. The user is able to analyse how well the company is able to manage its cash position, to generate cash to pay its debt obligations and fund its operating expenses. The Storyboard consists of 5 dashboards: Cash by Countries, Top 8 CashFlow Overview, CashFlow Overview, CashFlow Movement, CashFlow Movement Details
