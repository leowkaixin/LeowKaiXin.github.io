Commodity Web Scraping

Within this portfolio, I present a collection of projects tailored to stakeholders interested in the commodities market. These projects highlight my expertise in web scraping techniques, specifically geared towards extracting future values from commodity websites. The insights derived from these projects are invaluable for stakeholders, providing a forward-looking perspective on commodity prices and trends.
