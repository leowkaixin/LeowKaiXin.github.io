Financial Storyboards

In this portfolio, I showcase a series of financial storyboards I have created as a Group Data Analyst. These storyboards are designed to visually communicate complex financial data, making it easier for stakeholders to understand, analyze, and make informed decisions. Each storyboard provides a snapshot of key financial metrics, trends, and insights, helping organizations gain valuable insights from their financial data.

Disclaimer: Please be aware that the values have been masked for confidentiality reasons. The photos presented offer a general overview rather than a detailed in-depth perspective.

Storyboard 1: Balance Sheet

This storyboard provides an at-a-glance overview of a company's balance sheet. It highlights the proportions of assets, liabilities, and equity categories, allowing viewers to quickly assess the financial health and stability of the organization.

Storyboard 2: Profit and Loss

This storyboard focuses on the income statement, showcasing trends in revenue, expenses, and profitability over time. It helps stakeholders identify patterns and assess the company's financial performance.

Storyboard 3: CashFlow

In this storyboard, I analyze the cash flow statement to provide insights into a company's liquidity and cash management. It highlights cash inflows and outflows, helping organizations better understand their cash position.

Storyboard 4: OPEX (Operating Expenses)

This storyboard offers a comprehensive breakdown of OPEX by category, such as utilities, salaries, marketing, and more. It provides a clear visualization of how operating expenses are distributed, helping organizations identify areas where cost reduction or optimization is needed.

Storyboard 5: FX (Forex)

This storyboard offers a detailed analysis of currency pair trends, tracking the historical performance and fluctuations of various currency pairs. It allows viewers to assess how different currencies have fared against each other over time.

As a data analyst, I believe that effective data visualization is crucial for driving data-driven decision-making in the financial domain. These financial storyboards not only demonstrate my data analysis and visualization skills but also provide practical tools for organizations to gain valuable insights from their financial data. I'm excited to discuss how these storyboards can be customized to address specific financial analysis needs and contribute to informed decision-making.
