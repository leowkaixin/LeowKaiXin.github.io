About Me



I hold a B.Eng. with Honours (Merit), in Electrical and Electronic Engineering specializing in Infocommunication from Nanyang Technological University (NTU). Currently, working as a data analyst in a electronic waste company for more than year.

My role involves overseeing the design, operation, and maintenance of Finance Storyboards. I collaborate closely with the Group CFO and the Group Finance team to create, improve, and maintain the Group Financial Reporting Suite. This suite plays a crucial role in presenting essential financial insights about the organization, including indicators of its financial health growth, stability, risk assessment, and forward planning.

I am a self-driven individual, who strives for excellence in her endeavours. I find new challenges fascinating and see them as opportunities for personal growth. With my engineering background, I can critically assess situations and draw insightful conclusions. During my free time, I continuously enhance my skills through online courses such as Google Data Analytics and Foundations in Artificial Intelligence (AI). This piqued my curiosity to start exploring various visualization tools like PowerBI, Tableau and as well as engaging in AI projects.

Additionally, I also loves to go on hikes, occasionally bouldering and blazing the rubber in badminton.
